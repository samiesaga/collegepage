Reservations :

Admissions will be decided subject to the rules in force regarding reservations which are as under

Local & Non Local Candidates :

Admission to 85% of the available seats in every course shall be reserved in favour of the local candidates in relation to local area in respect of Osmania university and the remaining 15% of seats are left unreserved for which both local and non local candidates can compete.

BC, SC and ST Candidates :

Of the total of seats available in each course the following is the schedule of reservation of seats in these categories

category Percentage
Backward class 25%
Group-A 7%
Group-B 10%
Group-C 1%
Group-D 7%
Scheduled Caste 15%
Scheduled Tribe 6%

Woman :

The statutory rules for the reservation of seats will be followed.

Other Reserved Categories:

It covers Sports and Games / Extra-Curricular Activities / Physically Handicapped / NCC / Children of Ex-Servicemen and Army Personnel.

  • Sports/NCC/NSS: One seat for every block (30 seats) with a minimum of one seat taking the College as one Unit and it shall be reserved for Sports/NCC/NSS categories all put together in each of the courses.
  • Children of Armed personnel etc: One seat for every block (of 30 seats) with a minimum of one seat taking the college as one Unit and it shall be reserved for Physically Handicapped Candidates. For courses involving practicals their eligibility will be decided by a Committee appointed for this purpose.
