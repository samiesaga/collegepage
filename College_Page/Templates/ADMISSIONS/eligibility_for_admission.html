Eligibility for Admission :

Eligibility for U.G Admission:

The college is co-educational institute and admission is open to both boys and girls.

  • A student seeking admission to B.Com(computers) or B.Sc(MPC) course should have passed the intermediate examination conducted by the Board of Intermediate examination TS or any other examination recognized as equivalent thereto in the subjects concerned. However all Intermediate passed students are eligible to apply for B.Com(computers) Course Irrespective of the subjects is studied at the intermediate level.
  • A student seeking admission to B.Sc (MPC) I-year course should have passed and obtained at least 40% marks in the optional subjects at the qualifying examination. However for SC/ST category pass marks is the eligibility criterion.
  • Compartmental and instant examination candidates: candidates passing the qualifying examination complementary or through instant examination are treated alike and are also eligible to apply, but their cases will be considered provided;
  • Seats are available after accommodating one attempt candidates.
  • They are able to submit the memorandum of marks and other necessary documents before the prescribed date.

Eligibility for U.G Admission:

  • A student seeking admission to P.G courses have to appear Osmania University Common Entrance Test (OUCET) and admissions will be given through the counselling by the Osmnia University as per merit in OUCET.
