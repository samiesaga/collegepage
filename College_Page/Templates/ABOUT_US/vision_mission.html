Vision:

We aim to strive and uphold the values of humanity, nurturing through education, the next generation of nation builders.

Mission:

  • To impart quality education and create a healthy learning environment
  • To promote high standards of teaching and research, ethics, integrity and human values
  • To motivate students to acquire creative and critical skills
  • To inculcate the values and virtues of citizenship
  • To prepare students to meet the demands of new realms of global knowledge
  • To build and strengthen the region and the nation through pursuit of knowledge
